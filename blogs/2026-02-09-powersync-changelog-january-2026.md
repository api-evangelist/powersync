AI tools are moving at tremendous speed (whatever happened to “prompt engineering”?) and while we might not always be shipping bleeding-edge integrations with Ralph loops or Moltbots, we are closely monitoring the space and want to support developers using these tools. We’re starting by revamping the PowerSync CLI which will form the foundation for our plans in this space.
